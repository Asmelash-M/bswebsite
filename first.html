About Us

Welcome to Caffine Corner, where the rich aroma of freshly ground beans mingles with the warmth of community. Nestled in the heart of Addis Ababa, our coffee house is more than just a destination for exceptional brews; it's a haven for those who savor the simple pleasures of life. At Caffine Corner, we believe in the art of crafting the perfect cup, and our passion for coffee is evident in every pour. From the carefully sourced beans to the skilled baristas who bring them to life, we are dedicated to delivering a coffee experience that transcends the ordinary. Our story begins with a love for the bean and a desire to create a space where people can connect, unwind, and indulge in the finer things in life. The cozy ambiance of our coffee house invites you to escape the hustle and bustle of daily life and savor the moment with every sip. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community. Our beans are ethically sourced from the world's finest coffee-growing regions, ensuring that every cup reflects the care and attention invested in its creation. We are passionate about supporting local farmers and fostering a sense of global responsibility through our choices.But Caffine Corner is more than just a place to get your caffeine fix; it's a gathering spot for friends, colleagues, and fellow coffee enthusiasts. Whether you're seeking a quiet corner to work, catching up with friends over a latte, or simply enjoying a moment of solitude with a good book, our doors are always open to welcome you.
